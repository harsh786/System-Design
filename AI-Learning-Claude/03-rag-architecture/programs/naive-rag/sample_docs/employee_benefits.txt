NovaTech Solutions Employee Benefits and Policies

Vacation and Time Off:
All full-time employees receive 22 days of paid time off (PTO) per year. After 3 years of service, PTO increases to 27 days. After 5 years, it increases to 32 days. PTO does not roll over beyond 5 days into the next calendar year. Unused days beyond 5 are forfeited on December 31st.

Sick Leave:
Employees receive 10 paid sick days per year. Sick leave can be used for personal illness, medical appointments, or caring for an immediate family member. A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

Parental Leave:
NovaTech provides 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for all new parents, regardless of gender. This applies to birth, adoption, and surrogacy. An additional 4 weeks of unpaid leave may be requested. Parental leave must be taken within the first 12 months of the child's arrival.

Remote Work Policy:
Employees work from office on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday. Mondays and Fridays are remote-flexible. Employees may request up to 4 weeks of fully remote work per year for travel or personal reasons, subject to manager approval. International remote work requires prior approval from HR and Legal due to tax implications.

Health Insurance:
NovaTech covers 90% of premiums for employees and 75% for dependents. Plans include medical, dental, and vision. Employees can choose between an HMO and a PPO plan. Coverage begins on the first day of employment.

401(k) Retirement Plan:
NovaTech matches employee contributions up to 4% of base salary. Vesting is immediate. Employees are automatically enrolled at 3% contribution unless they opt out.
